# Clinical Validation Pathway (IRB & Regulatory Ready)

Phase 1 — Signal Equivalence
- Objective: demonstrate rPPG-derived HR and HRV equivalence vs gold-standard ECG across resting and paced breathing conditions.
- Metrics: Bland–Altman, Pearson r > 0.9, mean absolute error thresholds aligned to device spec.

Phase 2 — Cross-Sectional Correlation
- Objective: correlate rPPG biomarkers with DSM-5 based clinical instruments (PHQ-9, GAD-7, CAPS)
- Target: ROC-AUC > 0.82 for high-risk thresholds; Brier score < 0.15.

Phase 3 — Prospective Outcome
- Objective: 12-month prediction of clinically meaningful deterioration or improvement.
